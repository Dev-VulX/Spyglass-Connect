Spyglass Connect

Your Minecraft world, on your phone.

View your inventory, find items in any chest, locate structures, and explore an overhead map — all streamed from your PC over local WiFi. No cloud. No login. Just scan and play.

Download Spyglass Get Desktop App

Three Steps. That's It.

Launch on PC

Run Spyglass Connect on your desktop. It reads your Minecraft save files — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Scan QR Code

Open Spyglass on your phone and scan the QR code. Devices pair instantly with end-to-end encryption.

Play

Your world data streams to your phone in real-time. Auto-reconnects whenever you're on the same WiFi.

What You Can Do

Inventory Viewer

See your full inventory, armor, offhand, and ender chest. Every item links to the Spyglass database with full stats.

Chest Finder

Search for any item across every container in your world — chests, barrels, shulker boxes, hoppers. See coordinates and item counts.

Structure Locator

Find villages, temples, monuments, strongholds, and every generated structure. Distance and coordinates included.

Overhead Map

Interactive terrain map with structure markers and your player position. Zoom and pan to explore your entire world.

Character Viewer

Full player equipment and stats — armor, weapons, tools, and enchantments all visible at a glance.

Fully Encrypted

ECDH key exchange + AES-256-GCM encryption. Everything stays on your local network. Zero cloud, zero accounts.
