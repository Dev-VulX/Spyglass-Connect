Privacy Policy

Spyglass Connect — Minecraft Companion App • Last updated: March 4, 2026

Spyglass Connect is a free, ad-supported Android app created by Beryndil. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Spyglass does not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or account credentials. The app does not require you to create an account.

The following third-party services may collect data when enabled:

2. Google AdMob

Spyglass displays banner ads through Google AdMob. AdMob and its mediation partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address (for approximate location and ad targeting)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

If you decline personalized ads in the consent dialog or in Settings, Spyglass requests only non-personalized ads. Non-personalized ads do not use the advertising ID for targeting, but may still use your IP address for general geographic targeting.

For more details, see the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Firebase Analytics

When you accept Analytics, Spyglass uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage patterns, such as which screens are viewed and which features are used. This data helps improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

You can enable or disable analytics at any time in Settings > Privacy & Data.

4. Firebase Crashlytics

When you accept Crash Reporting, Spyglass uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash logs, stack traces, and device information (model, OS version) when the app crashes. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

You can enable or disable crash reporting at any time in Settings > Privacy & Data.

5. Firebase Performance Monitoring

When analytics consent is enabled, Firebase Performance Monitoring may collect app startup times and network request metrics. This data is anonymous and used to improve app performance.

6. Local Data Storage

All game data (blocks, items, recipes, mobs, etc.), your favorites, task lists, notes, waypoints, and settings are stored locally on your device using Room database and DataStore. This data is never transmitted to any server.

You can delete all locally stored user data at any time from Settings > Privacy & Data > Delete all my data.

7. Data Sync

Spyglass periodically checks a public GitHub repository for updated game data files. These requests are anonymous HTTP GET requests and do not transmit any personal or device information.

8. Your Choices

  • Consent dialog: On first launch, you can accept all, reject all, or customize which data collection features are enabled.
  • Settings: Toggle Analytics, Crash Reporting, and Personalized Ads on or off at any time.
  • Delete data: Remove all local user data (favorites, tasks, notes, waypoints, advancement progress) from Settings.
  • Advertising ID: You can reset or disable your device's advertising ID in your Android device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Spyglass is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app requests non-personalized ads by default, which is the privacy-safe option under COPPA.

10. Data Retention

Spyglass does not operate any servers and does not retain user data. Data collected by third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) is subject to Google's data retention policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
